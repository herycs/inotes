## 应用

### 阶乘

$$
n!=\left\{
\begin{aligned}
1 && n = 0 \\
n(n - 1) && n > 0 \\
\end{aligned}
\right.
$$

### Fibonacci数列

$$
F(x)=\left\{
\begin{aligned}
1 && n = 0 \\
1 && n = 1 \\
F(x - 1) + F(x - 2) && n > 1 \\
\end{aligned}
\right.
$$

### Ackerman函数

$$
\left\{

\begin{aligned}
A(1,0) & = 2 	& \\
A(0,m) & = 1 	 & m >= 0 \\
A(n,0) & = n + 2 & n >= 2 \\
A(n,m) & = A(A(n - 1, m), m - 1) & n,m >= 1 \\
\end{aligned}

\right.
$$
